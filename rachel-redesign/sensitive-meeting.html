Pre-meeting · marking sensitive

A toggle that sits on the meeting card before recording starts. Once on, the meeting carries the seal through every downstream surface.

When this is on, Docknote will still transcribe and synthesise as usual, but every surface that mentions this meeting will mask its body until you ask to see it. The seal travels with the meeting.

Mark sensitive on · seal will travel to Hearth, Murmur, Inbox

Sensitive meetings are excluded from any synthesis prompt that routes outside snapgpu. They never reach Claude even if you've opted into Claude routing for the rest of your meetings.
