Memory is not a book anymore. It is a view of every note from every meeting, drawn together in the shape your work already has. Engineering sits with engineering; ACME sits with ACME. The galaxy is the answer to "what do I have?" — you don't have to read a list, you can see the weight of the year at a glance.

Older notes dim, brighter dots are recent, and the lone anchor in each constellation is the meeting that gathered the most reference.
